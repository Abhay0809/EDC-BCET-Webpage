WHO WE ARE?




Established in 2008, Entrepreneurship Development Cell of Bengal College of Engneering and Technology is a renowned Entrepreneurship Cell and is counted among the best Entrepreneurial Development and Skill Development bodies in India. With an aim of motivating the student on entrepreneurship and thus creating entrepreneural minds among students, EDC, BCET has also worked in association with NEN(National Entrepreneurship Network) and is a hub of budding entrepreneurs. EDC, BCET is a fine blend of professionalism, intelligence, enthusiasm, initiatives, courtesy, hospitability, responsibility, brains and youth! Experienced entrepreneurs like our own chairman Shri S.K. Sharma forms an inspiration and a living example of how entrepreneurship can change society.
